Retirement Planning

Pressure-test a retirement plan before real life does.

Enter your current corpus, spending, recurring income and expense streams, and any one-time future events. The planner runs a Monte Carlo style projection to estimate how often your retirement plan stays funded through your target age.

Inputs

Plan details

Use annual amounts. Inflation-linked items are entered in today’s dollars and automatically grow with inflation in the model.

This is a planning aid, not financial advice. Taxes, sequence risk, fees, healthcare shocks, and lifestyle changes can materially change outcomes.

Results

Retirement confidence

Confidence means the share of simulation runs that never ran out of money and still met your desired ending balance.
