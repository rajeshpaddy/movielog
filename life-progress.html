A Good Life
Live with warmth, stay useful to others, protect your health, and spend your days on work, people, and moments you would gladly choose again.

A good life is rarely built from urgency alone. It usually comes from attention: loving people well, keeping your word, staying curious, moving your body, and remembering that ordinary days are most of life.
